About Us

Al-Maktabah is a comprehensive and free Islamic digital library designed to serve Muslims around the world with authentic, accessible, and multilingual resources — all without the need for sign-ups or payments.

We offer:

  • The Quran with multiple translations, detailed tafsirs, and a wide range of recitations by various qaris in different languages.
  • Extensive Hadith Collections, including Sahih Bukhari, Sahih Muslim, and more — available in multiple languages for deeper understanding.
  • A Rich Islamic Book Library, featuring works on theology, jurisprudence, history, spirituality, and more — from renowned scholars and authors across the Muslim world.
  • Advanced Quran Search, enabling users to search ayahs through text, keywords, or even images via OCR.
  • Islamic History, covering stories of prophets, the Seerah of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ, lives of the Sahabah, and historical developments through to the modern era.
  • User-Submitted Articles, allowing users to share insights and reflections on Islamic topics.
  • Interactive Quizzes to encourage learning across various themes in Islam.
  • Asma ul-Husna, with in-depth explanations of each of Allah’s 99 beautiful names.
  • Prayer Essentials, including a Qiblah compass, accurate prayer times, and a Hijri calendar.
  • Islamic Q&A, offering clear and concise answers to common and complex questions about the faith.

Our mission is to empower individuals with reliable Islamic knowledge in a simple, respectful, and distraction-free environment — for free, forever.
